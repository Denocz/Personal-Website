Cirriculum Vitae

The Mission statement

A committed individual who seeks to be one of the best information technology
engineers this world has ever seen. My mission is to do everything in my power
to comprehend the use of computers so that I can make the world a better place
by changing the way we think and manipulate the use of computers. My raison d'
etre is so that I can garner the potential to not just fix and build computers, but is
so that I can transform the lives of individuals, eradicating some of the pressing
global challenges that we face today. I aim to be at the forefront of technological
advancements

The Vision statement

My vision for the future is to be at the cutting edge of technological advancements
and innovation. In addition to that, I envision myself as not just Jamaica but one of
the world's top information technology engineers, designing a myriad of computer
hardware, and software that have the capacity to transform the lives of individuals.
Lastly, I want to educate individuals on how to understand and implement softwares
so that I can bring others into the fold so that they will also have the ability today
transform the world.

My Career choice

I have many career choices once I leave university by the Will of God. However, the
one that I aim to gravitate towards is an Information technology engineer. This is an
individual who is responsible for the implementing and design of computer hardware,
software and network. Anything that has to do with the logical design of computers.
Some of the job requirements and qualifications of an IT engineer that is essential are:
Bachelors Degree, 5 years of experience, knowledge of IT standards and practises
and Knowledge of IT strategy development, hardware and software systems.
